Purpose & Non-Claims (read first)

  • Simplified structural statics sandbox for a swivel/lever lift thought experiment.
  • Not a full rigging solver, pulley-angle model, historical reconstruction, or validated lifting simulation.
  • This version is focused on matching the right counterweight to the selected block weight and geometry.
  • It now explains how to fix a NO-GO setup so the user can reach a liftable configuration.
GO = lift balance and beam stress stay within this model’s limits.
NO-GO = more counterweight, better geometry, or stronger beam is needed.
