What is actually computed here
Weak-field clock-rate difference using the operational approximation:

dτ/dt ≈ 1 - GM/(r c^2) - v^2/(2 c^2) Clock-rate difference (satellite minus ground): Δ(dτ/dt) ≈ [GM/c^2] (1/r_ground - 1/r_sat) - (v_sat^2 - v_ground^2)/(2 c^2)
