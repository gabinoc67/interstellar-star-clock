Cash 5 Generator

This version is built for up to 100 past draws, applies a recent-draw boost, runs 3 rounds of 5 pretests, and then generates 5 final drawings.

Honest note: this is still a weighted pattern model, not a true prediction machine.

Recommended setup

1. Paste Past Draws

Format: one draw per line, newest first. You can paste 20, 50, or 100 lines. Best results usually come from 100.

Accepted formats examples:

12 19 25 30 35
04-04-2026: 12,19,25,30,35
