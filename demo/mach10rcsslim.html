Mission realism notes
This demo is more realistic than the earlier fast version because it no longer rushes the trip with an overly short mission clock. The aircraft now spends meaningful time in climb, acceleration, sustained cruise, controlled deceleration, and approach, while route completion is tied to integrated motion instead of a hardcoded finish timer.
