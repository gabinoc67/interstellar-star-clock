How to read this:
• q∞ is the main “air pushing back” reading.

Disclaimer: This software is a theory-only demonstration and instrumented browser simulation. It is not a certified flight model, not a validated CFD solver, not a real thermal protection design tool, not a wind-tunnel replacement, and not proof that sustained Mach 10 atmospheric cruise at 15,000 ft is physically achievable. The equations here are simplified engineering-style proxies intended to show how instrument readings could trend in a theoretical high-speed trip: atmosphere, Mach, dynamic pressure, stagnation temperature, total pressure rise, thermal loading, and local hotspot control. Use it as a conceptual visualization only.
