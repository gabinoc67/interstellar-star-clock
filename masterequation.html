Equations

Linearized GR + CST-Inspired Control Layer

Linearized Einstein equation in trace-reversed form
\[ \Box \bar{h}_{\mu\nu} = -\frac{16\pi G}{c^4} \left( T_{\mu\nu}^{\mathrm{class}} + \langle \hat{T}_{\mu\nu} \rangle + T_{\mu\nu}^{(\mathrm{vac})} + F_{\mu\nu}^{\mathrm{CST}} \right). \] \[ \bar{h}_{\mu\nu} = h_{\mu\nu} - \frac{1}{2}\eta_{\mu\nu} h, \qquad h = \eta^{\alpha\beta} h_{\alpha\beta}. \] Vacuum reference subtraction
\[ T_{\mu\nu}^{(\mathrm{vac})} = \langle 0|\hat{T}_{\mu\nu}|0\rangle - \langle 0|\hat{T}_{\mu\nu}|0\rangle_{\mathrm{flat}}. \] Toy quantum sector
\[ \dot{\hat{\rho}} = -\frac{i}{\hbar}[H_{\mathrm{eff}},\hat{\rho}] + \kappa \mathcal{D}[a]\hat{\rho} + \gamma_{\phi}\mathcal{D}[a^\dagger a]\hat{\rho}. \] This panel is a linearized and illustrative model, not a full nonlinear Einstein solver.
CST-style feedback law
\[ F_{\mu\nu}^{\mathrm{CST}} = -k_p\,\Delta h_{\mu\nu} -k_d\,\Delta \dot{h}_{\mu\nu}, \qquad \Delta h_{\mu\nu} = h_{\mu\nu}-h_{\mu\nu}^{\mathrm{target}}(t_{\mathrm{CST}}). \] Special relativity timing factor
\[ \gamma_{\mathrm{SR}}(v) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\dfrac{v^2}{c^2}}}, \qquad t'_{\mathrm{SR}} = \frac{t_{\mathrm{CST}}}{\gamma_{\mathrm{SR}}}. \] Schwarzschild-style clock factor
\[ \Gamma_{\mathrm{GR}}(r) = \sqrt{1-\frac{2GM}{rc^2}}, \qquad t'_{\mathrm{GR}} = t_{\mathrm{CST}}\Gamma_{\mathrm{GR}}. \] Sidereal drift
\[ \Delta t_{\mathrm{sid}} = N_{\mathrm{days}}(T_{\odot}-T_{\star}), \quad T_{\odot}\approx24\ \mathrm{h}, \quad T_{\star}\approx23^{\mathrm h}56^{\mathrm m}4.1^{\mathrm s}. \] Sky direction unit vector
\[ \mathbf{n}(\alpha,\delta) = (\cos\delta\cos\alpha,\ \cos\delta\sin\alpha,\ \sin\delta). \]
Relativistic energy reference
\[ E_{\mathrm{tot}}=\gamma_{\mathrm{SR}}mc^2, \qquad K=(\gamma_{\mathrm{SR}}-1)mc^2. \] At \(v=0.99c\), \(\gamma_{\mathrm{SR}}\approx 7.09\), so the kinetic energy is about \(6.09\,mc^2\). This dashboard does not simulate an actual rocket at \(0.99c\); it only provides a conceptual timing-and-residual sandbox.
