3. Generate 10 tickets at a time (inside 60-draw probabilities)

Uses probability weights from the official 60 draws. A memory system tracks how often each number has been used in past generations and penalizes over-used numbers, so under-used numbers get a boost and the shape stays closer to the real distribution (fixing overweight/underweight and reducing “never appears” problems).
Spread diagnostic will appear here (tight / balanced / loose) after generation.
⚠️ This simulator cannot defeat true lottery randomness. It only keeps your 10-ticket patterns inside the statistical shape of the 60 official Cash Five draws (odds, probabilities, and weights), while automatically reducing extreme over-weight / under-weight of individual numbers across many runs.
