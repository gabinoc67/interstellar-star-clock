Ship Region Explanation

Rear Zone
Behind the ship. The field bends upward and outward. In the visual this is the left side. This is your expansion side.
Center Bubble
The ship remains centered and static. The circular bubble marks the protected proper-region around the hull.
Front Zone
Ahead of the ship. The field bends downward and inward. In the visual this is the right side. This is your contraction side.
Line Motion
The lines animate across the bubble to show moving field structure while the ship does not translate on screen.

Trend Plot

Green = speed fraction. Yellow = remaining energy mass fraction. Blue = CST factor fraction. This is a compact live trend strip so the panels stay smaller.
Real side uses standard timing and relativistic benchmark equations. Theory side is a visualization and control-layer model for front/back bending, tunnel-path reduction, and CST ship-time behavior. It is not a validated warp solver.
