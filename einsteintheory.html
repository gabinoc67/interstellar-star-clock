Einstein–Casanova Unified Action — Theory Explanation

The Einstein–Casanova Unified Action proposes that gravity, gauge fields, dark matter, dark energy, vacuum energy, warp structures, and consciousness coupling can be treated as interacting sectors of one larger framework. This simulator is a sandbox that lets you tune those sectors and observe how dominance and stability shift as the environment changes.

The output is not a proof of physical law. It is a structured scoring model that helps you compare regimes, visualize balance across sectors, and track repeated test setups. In that sense, it is a research playground for exploring how classical and exotic contributions may interact under different assumed conditions.
