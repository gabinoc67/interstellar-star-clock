Step-by-step

  1. Open the interface. Confirm primary status lights are green and that the navigation module is responsive.
  2. Select destination. Use the destination field/list/map. Double-check coordinates before engaging.
  3. Pre-engage check. Verify engine temperature/pressure within limits, power available, and clocks synced.
  4. Engage the engine. Click Engage / Start Engine. Expect live updates across status, meters, and distance.
  5. Track progress. Watch engine clocks, miles, and any ETA/course indicators. Yellow/red states mean ‚Äúadjust or stop.‚Äù
  6. Hazard awareness. If proximity warnings appear, either adjust course or Stop until back within safe distance.
  7. Disengage. Click Stop / Shutdown. Wait for readings to settle before setting a new destination.
