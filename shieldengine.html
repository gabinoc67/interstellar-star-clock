    Step-by-Step Instructions
    1. Pick Date, then select Origin and Destination.
    2. Set Warp. Higher warp makes the map movement and engine activity stronger.
    3. Adjust Shield Power and Mode.
    4. Press ENGAGE to start a new live trip.
    5. Press HOLD to freeze travel and both clocks.
    6. Press Reset Route to fully clear the trip and start fresh.
