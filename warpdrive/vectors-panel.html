Math (implemented)

Proper Motion (fallback): μ≈(v⊥/d)·(rad→mas/yr), from Earth-relative tangential speed if pm not provided.

Cosmology: vrec=H₀·D (km/s) for D in Mpc; z≈v/c for small z. Δ(SE) shows Sun–Earth range in ly.
Uncertainty: σr≈|v|·Δt from vector timestamp age (km), “Last Update” shown in CST.
