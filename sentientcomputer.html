      What this fixed version now does

      This version removes the fake-looking logic chain and replaces it with a real one. Train/Error is calculated first from scenario traits. That result then feeds Positive/Negative. Positive/Negative then shapes Mood. The final YES/NO is produced from a deterministic weighted score compared against a threshold for that scenario.

      In other words, the program is no longer pretending that earlier stages matter. They now directly influence the final outcome.
