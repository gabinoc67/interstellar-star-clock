Interstellar Time — Revolutions Since Day 0

Each planet starts counting after its formation offset from Day 0. “Orbits completed” means full revolutions around the Sun since formation. “Current orbit” shows the active revolution and the percent progress through it.

Notes

  • Sun shows age since Day 0 only. It does not orbit the Sun.
  • Days / year baseline is the conversion scale for the IST clock.
  • Cosmic offset shifts only the current orbit phase, not the total age.
  • This version updates on a stable timer instead of an animation loop.
