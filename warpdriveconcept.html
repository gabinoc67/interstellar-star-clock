Live Equations Used in This Demo

eta = (Rb / R)^2
Vb = PI * Rb^2 * L
dt_CST = L / c_eff
v_eff = v_ship + v_bubble
rho = A*photons - B*casimir + C*entanglement - D*noise + E*coherence
p_r = p_theta = p_phi = w * rho
stability = function(eta target, field balance, radius fit, damping, noise)

These are still demo equations and normalized readouts. The goal here is consistency between what you type, what the field shows, and what the animation uses.

What This Version Fixes
