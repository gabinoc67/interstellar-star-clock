Limits & Risks

Scope: This site presents planning tools and hypotheses. It does not claim a proven, energy-realizable macroscopic warp tunnel today.

Energy & Stress–Energy

  • Feasible macroscopic field configurations remain unproven; positive‑energy proposals are under theoretical study.
  • Engineering questions: generation, containment, radiators, materials, and control surfaces.

Horizons, Causality & Global Structure

  • Effective superluminal motion can raise causality concerns depending on the metric and global topology.
  • Reachability in expanding space is treated with clear math (compare v_rec = H0·D0 to v_eff), but causal structure depends on the full solution.

Stability & Quantum Inequalities

  • Stability under perturbations is an open problem.
  • Quantum inequality bounds may constrain energy densities and durations.

Environment & Shielding

  • Dust/plasma interaction, radiation, charging, and EMI require robust mitigation (e.g., Arrow Shield concepts).
  • Operational safety requires sensing, range-gated actuation, and fail‑safes.

Ethics & Test Pathway

  • Testing should begin in small, sealed environments; publish data for open verification.
  • International collaboration encouraged; comply with safety standards and export controls.

What Is Claimed Here

  • Transparent planning math for expansion: reachability, time‑on‑target, and speed requirements.
  • CST timing + circle‑space routing as a navigation framework.
  • Reproducible inputs/outputs (JSON) for peer review.
