What this dashboard is and is not

The astronomy panels below are approximate solar-system calculations based on orbital elements and a Kepler solver. They are useful for a live educational dashboard and for visual understanding of orbital phase, heliocentric distance, and Earth-to-planet geometry.

The Interstellar Time, Stardate, and Cosmic Stardate panels are custom stylized displays. They are intentionally shown as non-standard displays and should not be interpreted as official astronomical time standards.
