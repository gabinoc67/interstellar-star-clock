Important Accuracy Notes

This version is more accurate than the earlier one because it takes direct birth-date and birth-time input and keeps the current UTC clock live after calculation. Zodiac sign is determined from the birth date. Moon phase is an estimate based on the selected date and synodic lunar cycle. Historical daylight savings and regional timezone changes are still not auto-detected, so the best result comes from entering the exact UTC offset that applied at the birthplace on the birth date.

The CST cosmic offset remains a comparison control layer and is shown separately from the raw astronomical values.
