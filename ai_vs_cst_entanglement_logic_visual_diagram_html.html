Notes

Sophie‑style agents here means enterprise multimodal assistants that run on classical silicon and binary logic. They are powerful at perception and language via deep learning. They do not inherently use quantum hardware or quantum logic.

SPR‑C CST logic models decisions as balance operations under a global timing signal (Cosmic Synchronization Time). The flip‑gate samples both memory banks; when the CST phase aligns and the expected energy cost is below a threshold, the gate commits to an action. This mirrors biological oscillations (e.g., 40‑Hz gamma) in spirit, not exact physiology.
