W-4 PDF Download and Extra Withholding Explanation

Moved to the bottom as requested
Before downloading the form, the customer or user should first fill out Step 1 with name, address, Social Security number, and filing status. If the person has only one job and no special adjustments, they may skip to Step 5 after Step 1, but if they want more federal tax taken out so they do not owe taxes and want a bigger refund, the main place to enter an extra amount is Step 4(c), which is the line for extra withholding each pay period. This dashboard estimates the extra withholding by comparing the estimated taxes to the current tax withholding and dividing the difference by 12 months. Example: if estimated taxes are $3,580.00 and only $700.00 is being withheld, the difference is $2,880.00, and $2,880.00 ÷ 12 = $240.00 more per month to withhold for a better refund target. If estimated taxes are fully covered or overpaid already, the extra monthly withholding can stay blank or zero.
