👴 Social Security Explanation

Simple Social Security Retirement Explanation

If a person is under full retirement age and works while receiving Social Security retirement, benefits can be reduced under the annual earnings test. In 2026, this planner uses $24,480 for someone below full retirement age all year and $65,160 for the year full retirement age is reached before the FRA month.
