Family Tax & Budget Impact Dashboard

This premium planning dashboard helps customers see whether they may need more taxes taken out during the year, whether they may be headed toward owing taxes or receiving a refund, and whether their family budget is under control after the impact of food, tariffs, inflation, and higher prices.

The customer enters annual gross income, payroll deductions, estimated tax deductions, taxes already withdrawn, and family budget information. The dashboard then estimates tax pressure, refund pressure, monthly affordability, and whether adding extra withholding on W-4 Step 4(c) may fit within the budget.

Credit & Refund Explanation
