🧠 Plain-Language Guidance

Professional Guidance, Credit Explanation, and Disclaimer

Run the dashboard to see whether the customer may need extra withholding on W-4 Step 4(c).
A customer may still receive a refund from child credits, dependent credits, teacher credits, or other credits. However, if not enough taxes were withdrawn during the year, part of those credits may first go toward taxes still owed. That can reduce the final refund amount.
Professional disclaimer:
This dashboard is a simplified educational estimate only. It is not legal, tax, accounting, or financial advice. It does not replace a full tax return, Schedule 8812 worksheets, ACTC calculations, EITC calculations, itemized deductions, state returns, or special tax situations. Final decisions should always be reviewed with a qualified tax accountant, enrolled agent, CPA, or other tax professional because every family’s tax situation is different and can be more complex than a simplified dashboard estimate.
