W-4 PDF Download and Extra Withholding Explanation

Download the 2026 Form W-4 and read where to enter extra withholding
Before downloading the form, the customer or user should first fill out Step 1 with name, address, Social Security number, and filing status. If the person has only one job and no special adjustments, they may skip to Step 5 after Step 1, but if they want more federal tax taken out so they do not owe taxes and want a bigger refund, the main place to enter an extra amount is Step 4(c), which is the line for extra withholding each pay period. This dashboard now estimates the extra withholding by comparing the estimated taxes to the current tax withholding and dividing the difference by 12 months. Example: if estimated taxes are $3,580 and only $700 is being withheld, the difference is $2,880, and $2,880 ÷ 12 = $240 more per month to withhold for a better refund target. If estimated taxes are fully covered or overpaid already, the extra monthly withholding can stay blank or zero.
