W-4 PDF Download and Extra Withholding Explanation

Download the 2026 Form W-4 and read where to enter extra withholding
Before downloading the form, the customer or user should first fill out Step 1 with name, address, Social Security number, and filing status. If the person has only one job and no special adjustments, they may skip to Step 5 after Step 1, but if they want federal taxes taken out more evenly so they do not owe at tax time, the main place to enter an extra amount is Step 4(c), which is the line for extra withholding each pay period. If your tax results show that you will still owe taxes, take the extra amount needed for the year, divide it by the number of pay periods left, and place that extra amount on W-4 Step 4(c). If your results show that enough taxes are already being withheld and you do not expect to owe, you can leave Step 4(c) blank. If claiming children or other dependents in Step 3 lowers withholding too much and reduces the tax being taken out, that can also reduce your refund or cause a balance due, so in that case you can enter an additional amount in Step 4(c) to offset the credits and have more tax withheld during the year. If the person has multiple jobs or a working spouse, they should also review Step 2, because total household income can change the correct withholding amount. The goal is to use the W-4 so the paycheck withholding is closer to the actual tax due for the year, helping the person avoid owing taxes and, if desired, increase the chance of a refund.
