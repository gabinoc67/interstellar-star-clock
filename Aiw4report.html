Important: This estimator keeps household budget cash separate from federal tax estimation. Social Security retirement is not included in household net cash on this version. It is shown only to warn retirees that if they are under full retirement age and earn over the yearly earnings-test limit, part of their future Social Security checks may be reduced next year. That reduction is not permanently lost forever, because SSA later recalculates benefits when full retirement age is reached.
