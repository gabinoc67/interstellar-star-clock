W-4 Explanation and Download

Use the corrected extra withholding result
This dashboard uses the rule: Recommended Extra Tax Withholding Per Month = Estimated Owed Taxes ÷ 12. If the customer is expected to still owe taxes at the end of the year after credits and withholding, the dashboard spreads that shortage across 12 months. If the customer is already fully covered, the extra amount stays at zero.

Customer story: less withholding now = more take-home pay now, but more chance of a smaller refund or tax bill later; more withholding now = less take-home pay now, but safer year-end tax result. Inflation and price pressure then reduce the monthly leftover even more.
