Program Explanation

This is a theoretical educational simulator. It models live clock synchronization, Earth-to-Mars travel, phase-controlled pulse chambers, throttle power, rearward exhaust, dual engine routing, radiation shielding, Whipple impact protection, and a 12-circle pulse-through warp throttle that does not store bulk reaction energy on the ship.
