Stage Process Explanation

Stage 1

Booster / air takeoff. Reads launch weather, air density, wind, thrust, heat, and vibration.

Stage 2

Hybrid main engine. Oxidizer flow increases while damping keeps pressure waves controlled.

Stage 3

Upper atmosphere. Stored oxidizer, cooling, chamber pressure, and nozzle expansion dominate.

Stage 4

Vacuum space stage. No air is used. Thrust comes from carried propellant and exhaust velocity.

This is an educational simulation. The force, speed, temperature, damping, and curvature readings are simplified display values, not certified flight data.
