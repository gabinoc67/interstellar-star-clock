Paste the historical machine-order file with the oldest draw at the top and the newest draw at the bottom. The history remains in that same order. Recent scoring windows and the newest 40 point sequences are read from the bottom of the stored history.

Step 1 — Paste Historical Machine-Order Draws

Paste one draw per line. The oldest draw must be at the top and the newest draw at the bottom. The first number is Column 1 and the fifth number is Column 5. Spaces, tabs, commas, semicolons, pipes, and hyphens are accepted.

Historical order:
Oldest draw at the top → newest draw at the bottom.
No internal reversal is performed. The last rows are treated as the newest draws.
Rotation rule: every point group has an independent cursor in every column. Repeated numbers inside a generated row are preserved and highlighted.
Paste at least 40 valid historical draws and select Analyze History.
