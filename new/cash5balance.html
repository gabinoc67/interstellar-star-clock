Cash 5 Balanced Coverage Generator

This program does not predict the lottery. It analyzes draw history, builds balanced number zones, avoids cloned tickets, adds controlled randomness, and now includes Zone Rotation, Missing Number Insurance, and Neighbor Bridge logic.

Draw History Input

Paste at least 50 to 100 past Cash 5 draws. One draw per line. Use spaces, commas, tabs, or dashes. Example: 13 14 23 24 28

Important: This is a coverage and distribution tool, not a guaranteed prediction tool. The new upgrade forces wider number rotation so numbers like 11 and 12 are less likely to be absent.

Algorithm Settings

Program Equations Used Before Ticket Output

Frequency Score: how often each number appeared in the uploaded history.

Recency Score: newer draws receive a small weight so the program can detect recent movement without overfitting.

Zone Rotation: forces all zones to rotate numbers so no zone becomes too narrow.

Missing Number Insurance: after selection, the program repairs coverage gaps by injecting absent or low-use numbers.

Neighbor Bridge: numbers near active numbers receive a small bridge boost. Example: 9 can help 10 or 11 enter the pool.

Anti-Clone Rule: a new ticket is rejected if it shares more than the clone limit with selected tickets.
