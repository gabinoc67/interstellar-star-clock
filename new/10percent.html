Disclaimer

This is a theoretical educational demo, not a medical test, IQ test, diagnosis, or neuroscience measurement device. It does not measure real neurons or synapses. It only models the theory that learning improves through better connection quality, recall, feedback, sleep, challenge, and repeated practice.

Short Theory Explanation

Based on the Academia paper logic, intelligence growth is modeled as better brain-network efficiency, not simply “using more of the brain.” The simulator treats practice, recall, feedback, sleep, and challenge as training forces that improve connection quality.

The test lets the user take 20 questions, review the correct answers, train weak areas, then repeat the test. Improvement is shown as a theory-based learning change, not a medical measurement.

Core Rule

You do not upgrade intelligence by increasing total connections.

You upgrade performance by improving connection quality, organization, timing, recall, and efficiency.

Training Guidance

  • Use recall instead of only rereading.

  • Use moderate challenge, not impossible challenge.
  • Repeat the test to compare first and second attempt.
