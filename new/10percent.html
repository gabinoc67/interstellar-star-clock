Disclaimer

This is a theoretical educational demo, not a medical test, IQ test, diagnosis, or neuroscience measurement device.

It does not measure real neurons or synapses. It models the theory that learning improves through practice, recall, feedback, sleep, challenge, and repeated training.

Short Theory Explanation

Based on the Academia paper logic, intelligence growth is modeled as better brain-network efficiency, not simply “using more of the brain.”

The simulator lets the user answer 20 questions, review correct answers, train weak areas, and repeat the test to compare improvement.

Core Rule

You do not upgrade intelligence by increasing total connections.

You upgrade performance by improving connection quality, organization, timing, recall, and efficiency.
