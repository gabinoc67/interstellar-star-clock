Short Theory Explanation

Intelligence is not based on neuron quantity (~86 billion), but on neural efficiency, organization, and synchronization over time.

CST defines intelligence as a time-evolving network optimization process driven by repeated activation, environmental adaptation, and learning feedback.

Growth Model: Practice × Recall × Feedback × Sleep × Challenge

Core Rule

You do not upgrade intelligence by increasing total connections.

You upgrade performance by improving efficiency, organization, and timing of neural pathways.
