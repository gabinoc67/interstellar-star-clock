Simulation Purpose

This simulator compares ancient sites as Earth-sky measurement stations. It shows how local geography, flat horizon, clear sky, water leveling, Sun shadow, Moon cycles, stars, and calendar continuity could limit or improve precision. CST Cosmic Clock is used here as a comparison framework, not as a claim of magic technology.
