NDV / DCI Formula Logic

NDV = current-position number − previous-position number.

Near-zero NDV marks anchor persistence.

DCI = average spacing between sorted draw numbers.

Low DCI = compressed/cluster draw.
Medium DCI = hybrid draw.
High DCI = spread draw.
