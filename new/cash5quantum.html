Odd / Even Pattern Rules

3 odd / 2 even and 2 odd / 3 even are the preferred patterns. These two patterns usually dominate because balanced combinations occur much more often than all-odd or all-even combinations. All odd or all even is rare compared with mixed combinations. This program scores: • 3/2 and 2/3 as strongest • 4/1 and 1/4 as weaker • 5/0 and 0/5 as rare
