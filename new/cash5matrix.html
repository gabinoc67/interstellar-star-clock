8. Point and Exposure System

Independent Number-Column Entries

Number 01 in Column 1 is treated separately from Number 01 in Column 3, Column 4, or Column 5. Every entry receives its own points, targets, and actual usage.

First Section Tickets 1–5. Strong pressure toward high-point Top entries, while still allowing controlled rotation.
Middle Section Tickets 6–35. Stronger Middle-entry rotation with continued Top and Least coverage.
Last Section Tickets 36–40. Least entries receive stronger return pressure, while high-point entries can reappear.

The program converts the weighted points into exact exposure targets. Every column receives exactly 5 placements in Tickets 1–5, exactly 30 placements in Tickets 6–35, and exactly 5 placements in Tickets 36–40.
