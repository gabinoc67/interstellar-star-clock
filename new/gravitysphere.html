Purpose and Theory Explanation

This program is a theoretical CST Bell Sphere control demo. It shows how separate ship sections could use local equilibrium spheres to keep crew gravity near Earth gravity while the spacecraft travels from Earth to Mars.

In this model, each compartment has its own sensor loop. If measured gravity drops, the sphere increases acceleration support. If gravity rises too high, the sphere reduces support. The goal is not time travel. The goal is stable crew weight, stable timing, stable navigation, and safer field balance.

Disclaimer: This is a conceptual educational simulator. It does not prove artificial gravity, warp drive, or faster-than-light propulsion. It is a logic model based on field equilibrium, Newton’s second law, timing synchronization, and control feedback.

CST Bell Sphere Principle: keep every ship section synchronized, balanced, and returned to Earth-gravity equilibrium.
