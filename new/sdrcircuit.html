Simulation Intention

This simulator visualizes the SDR sequence as presented within the framework itself. The model begins from 1 as source identity, allows deviation to appear as relational separation, shows how addition can recover φ, and shows how division restores exact unity.

Core thesis: Addition can recover φ, but division restores 1.

Return is recognition by division. The expression measures itself by itself and restores 1.

Scientific and Mathematical Disclaimer: This simulator distinguishes between the formal mathematical sequence and the semantic interpretations proposed within the SDR framework. Standard mathematics confirms identities such as 1 × 1 = 1 and n ÷ n = 1 for nonzero n. The SDR meanings of source, deviation, recognition, return, and Love are presented as part of the author’s proposed framework.
