Structured around TREC REI 7-6 with an integrated study-book reference guide

How to use the REI 7-6 status boxes

IInspected
NINot Inspected
NPNot Present
DDeficient
  • Check the appropriate status box for every report item.
  • Explain findings under the corresponding section.
  • More than one status may be selected when the written explanation supports the combination.
  • When both I and NI are selected, explain which portions were and were not inspected and why.
  • For a single-item inspection, keep all required sections and mark uninspected items NI with an explanation.

Purpose and limitations

This workspace is intended to organize observations from a visual survey and basic performance evaluation of present, visible, and accessible systems and components. It is not a technically exhaustive inspection, a code-compliance inspection, an appraisal, or a warranty.

Drafting and reference aid only. Verify the final report against the current official TREC form, Standards of Practice, and your inspection agreement before delivery.
