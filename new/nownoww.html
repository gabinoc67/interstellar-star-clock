Main Mathematical Idea
Each observer has local time, signal delay, and relativistic drift.

ΔT = TEarth − TVoyager − Tdelay

CCST = Σ wi(Ti − Tref)²

The CST engine tries to reduce the mismatch between Earth, Sun, Voyager 1, Voyager 2, and the atomic reference.
Meaning
The Sun is not seen at the instant the light leaves the Sun. Earth receives the Sun ray later. That delayed information is OTBT. The observer reconstructs a usable NOWW using UTC, atomic time, CST correction, and interstellar timing references.
