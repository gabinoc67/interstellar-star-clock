Mission & Open Collaboration

Goal: Advance a practical path toward safe, verifiable faster‑than‑light–adjacent propulsion research by combining new geometric tunnel models ("Warp Tunnels") with known physics and rigorous instrumentation.

Why: This project is not about glory or fame. It’s about resilience for humankind—preparing for solar limits, asteroid risk, and other existential threats—while expanding opportunity: interplanetary resources can augment (not replace) today’s energy sector by enabling exploration for hydrocarbons, minerals, and volatiles off‑world.

How to engage: I do not currently operate a lab. I seek collaboration with universities, national labs, and agencies (NASA, ESA, CNSA) and private partners to design instrumented, small‑scale experiments that test field geometry, shielding effects, and navigation timing without making extraordinary claims.
