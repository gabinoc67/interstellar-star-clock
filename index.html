How the Arrow Shield Uses Photons

Photons carry momentum p = E/c. Natural starlight is too weak to push debris, so the Arrow Shield uses onboard lasers/microwave three ways:

  1. Ionization cone to charge dust, then magnetic fields deflect grains.
  2. Ablation nudges on larger bits to impart lateral Δv.
  3. Range-gated control to widen the cone, pulse, or adjust heading as needed.
