Disclaimer

Important research notice

This website contains a mix of theoretical research, conceptual models, exploratory simulations, mathematical proposals, and structured interpretation. Some material may reference established scientific language or known equations, but not all content should be interpreted as experimentally validated physics or accepted scientific consensus.

Simulations presented on this site are exploratory tools intended to visualize ideas, investigate possibilities, and support discussion. They do not by themselves constitute proof of physical correctness, engineering feasibility, or real-world performance. Visitors are encouraged to evaluate all material critically and distinguish between grounded analysis, working theory, and speculative proposal.
