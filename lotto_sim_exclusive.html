Lotto Texas Verifier Engine

This page is a method verifier, not a claim of beating randomness. It tests a fixed rule-set on held-out future draws, compares it against a random baseline, calculates an empirical p-value, freezes the rules before testing, stores an audit log, and then generates a forecast using only the final available training window. Lotto Texas uses 6 numbers from 1 to 54.
