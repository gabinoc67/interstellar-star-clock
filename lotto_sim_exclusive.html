Lotto Texas Verified Prediction Verifier

This page is designed as a verifier. It uses a fixed rule-set, runs a rolling out-of-sample backtest on embedded official draw history, compares performance against random tickets, calculates a significance estimate, and then generates 10 forecast lines from the latest training window. It does not claim to defeat lottery randomness.
