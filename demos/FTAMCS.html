What This Program Is About

This program is a control-and-visualization simulation for an internal cone engine concept. It combines tile thrust, synchronization, resonance, cone efficiency, and coupling into one live effective thrust estimate.
Math used:

Tile-adjusted thrust = base thrust × sync factor.
Total thrust = tile-adjusted thrust × number of tiles × resonance × cone efficiency factor × coupling factor.
Acceleration = force ÷ mass.
Direction uses cosine and sine for X and Y vector components.
Why it now looks live:

A continuous animation loop updates the visuals every frame, instead of only redrawing when the slider changes.
