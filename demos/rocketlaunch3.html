What this simulation is doing

This is a toy corridor ascent from Point A to Point B in East–Up (x–y). It blends Earth rotation, q compression, drag/lift, a simple heat proxy + coolant, and a 5-stage burn.
  • Point A (latitude) changes the rotation bonus v_rot (free eastward velocity).
  • Point B (vector target) creates a stable “corridor” aimpoint instead of “straight up.”
  • Curvature control helps manage q/heat peaks and reduces wasted steering (toy framing).
  • CST + UTC clocks show timing windows (not claiming spacetime proof—just control/timing).
Bold atmosphere lines at 10/30/60/100/150/200 km. Point A & B are circles. Stage ends are marked on the path.
