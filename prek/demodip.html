1st Grade Early Literacy Practice

Timed early literacy practice inspired by a simple first-format screening style. This is a classroom-style practice tool for skill development and review. It is not an official DIBELS assessment.
Practice Notice
This tool is for literacy fluency practice only. Scores are classroom-style indicators, not official benchmark decisions.

Setup

How it works
1. Choose a measure and school window.
2. Start the timer.
3. Show the student the prompt on screen.
4. Teacher marks correct or incorrect.
5. The next item appears immediately.

Measures
LNF = student names letters.
FSF = student says the first sound in a word.
PSF = student breaks a word into sounds.
Suggested classroom use: adult-guided one-on-one practice.
