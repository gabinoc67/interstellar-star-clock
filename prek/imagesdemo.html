Pre-K & Kinder Picture Vocabulary Explorer

This program uses 250 visual picture cards by category to help students learn vocabulary through recognition, naming, category sorting, and spoken-word support. Tap any card or speaker button to hear the word.

It is built for classroom practice, intervention, centers, and home reinforcement. The visuals are large, simple, and student-friendly so learners can focus on picture meaning first and word recognition second.
