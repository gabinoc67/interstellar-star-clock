Teacher Practice Suite

One-on-one teacher testing format. Student looks at the picture or prompt, answers aloud, and teacher clicks Correct or Incorrect. Includes a print report for the session.

Setup

How it works
1. Choose student, grade, window, and category.
2. Start the session.
3. Teacher shows the student the screen prompt or picture.
4. Student answers aloud.
5. Teacher clicks Correct or Incorrect.
6. Print the report when finished.
Disclaimer
This program is a classroom-style teacher practice tool. It is not an official test, not a diagnostic decision tool, and not the original assessment program. Question banks, picture prompts, and scoring guides are simplified practice approximations and should be reviewed by a teacher or adult before instructional or reporting use.
