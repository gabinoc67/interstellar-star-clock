Early Literacy Teacher Practice

One-on-one teacher testing format for Letter Naming Fluency, First Sound Fluency, and Phoneme Segmentation Fluency. Teacher asks the student, marks correct or incorrect, and prints a report at the end.

Setup

How it works
1. Choose a measure and school window.
2. Start the timer.
3. Teacher asks the student to respond to the item on screen.
4. Teacher clicks Correct or Incorrect.
5. The next item appears immediately.

Measures
LNF = student names letters.
FSF = student says the first sound in a word.
PSF = student breaks a word into sounds.

Suggested classroom use: adult-guided one-on-one practice.
Disclaimer
This program is a classroom-style teacher practice tool. It is not an official test, not a diagnostic decision tool, and not the original assessment program. Benchmark guides and prompts are simplified practice approximations and should be reviewed by a teacher or adult before instructional or reporting use.
