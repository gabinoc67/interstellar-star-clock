Three-Engine Mars Curvature Simulation

Simple theory demo using a tug-boat hybrid architecture. Nuclear acts as the prime mover, Electric/Ion acts as the steady tug, and Plasma acts as the burst tug. Local motion stays subluminal while effective travel changes through corridor compression from Earth to Mars. Panels are grouped into live readings and equations, with atomic Earth time, rocket CST time, and interstellar Voyager timing.
